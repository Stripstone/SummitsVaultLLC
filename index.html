About The Firm

SummitsVault LLC is a precious metals buying, selling, and trading firm dedicated to secure, transparent, and efficient transactions. We serve as a reliable partner for refineries, estate liquidators, and private investors.

Operating with strict adherence to industry standards, our mission is to provide competitive market pricing and seamless logistical handling for bulk bullion and numismatic assets. Looking ahead, SummitsVault is expanding its infrastructure to include educational tools and digital asset integration.

Anti-Money Laundering

Effective Date: January 2026

SummitsVault LLC is committed to conducting business in a lawful, ethical, and transparent manner and to discouraging the use of its services for money laundering, terrorist financing, or other illicit activities.

Business Scope and Risk Profile

SummitsVault LLC primarily acquires precious metals and sells to established refineries, coin dealers, and recognized auction or trading platforms. Based on the nature and scale of its operations, the Company considers its current money laundering risk profile to be limited.

Risk-Based Approach

The Company maintains reasonable, risk-based procedures designed to identify and mitigate potential illicit activity, appropriate to the size, complexity, and stage of its business. Controls are implemented in proportion to operational risk and may evolve as the business grows.

Customer and Counterparty Practices

SummitsVault LLC generally transacts with known, reputable counterparties and maintains basic records related to acquisitions, sales, and payments. The Company does not knowingly engage in transactions involving unlawful proceeds.

Cash Transactions

The Company seeks to limit exposure to high-risk transactions, including large or anonymous cash transactions, and complies with applicable reporting and recordkeeping requirements when required by law.

Record Keeping

Transaction records, invoices, and related documentation are retained for business and compliance purposes in accordance with applicable laws and industry practices.

Suspicious Activity

If a transaction appears unusual or inconsistent with normal business activity, the Company may decline to proceed and take appropriate action consistent with applicable legal obligations.
